Kumaraguru College of Technology (KCT), established in 1984, is a private engineering institution located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. The college operates under the auspices of the Ramanandha Adigalar Foundation, a charitable educational trust of the Sakthi Group. Situated on a 156-acre campus in Coimbatore's IT corridor, KCT is affiliated with Anna University, Chennai, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
